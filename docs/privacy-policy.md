# Privacy Policy for ChatMe

**Effective date:** 2026-04-16

ChatMe (“the app”) is a chat application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## Information we collect

- **Information you provide**
  - **Messages and content you submit**: chat messages and any content you send inside the app.
  - **Support/contact information** (if you contact us): the email address and details you provide.

- **Information collected automatically**
  - **Basic device/app data**: such as device model, operating system version, app version, and crash/diagnostic information. This is used to keep the app stable and improve performance.

## How we use information

- **Provide the service**: deliver chat functionality and sync content across your devices (if applicable).
- **Maintain and improve the app**: diagnose bugs, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.
- **Support**: respond to questions and requests.

## Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information only in these cases:

- **Service providers**: hosting, analytics, crash reporting, and other providers that help operate the app. They may process information on our behalf under their own terms and privacy policies.
- **Legal requirements**: if required to comply with applicable law, regulation, or legal process.
- **Safety**: to protect users, prevent fraud/abuse, or address security issues.

## Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide the service and for legitimate business purposes (such as security, compliance, and troubleshooting). You may request deletion as described below.

## Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Your choices

- **Uninstall**: you can uninstall the app at any time.
- **Deletion request**: you can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

## Children’s privacy

ChatMe is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above when changes are made.
