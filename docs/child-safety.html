ChatMe Child Safety Standards (CSAE)

Last updated: 2026-04-16

ChatMe is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We do not allow content that sexualizes or exploits children, including child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

Prohibited content

  • Any CSAM/CSAE content
  • Sexual content involving minors or content that depicts or promotes the exploitation of children
  • Grooming, solicitation, or attempted exploitation of minors

Reporting

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, contact your local emergency services right away.

To report CSAE/CSAM concerns related to ChatMe, contact us at: davidzdravkovic21@gmail.com

Response and compliance

  • We will review CSAE/CSAM reports and take appropriate action, which may include content removal and account restriction.
  • Where required, we will report apparent CSAM to relevant authorities and/or organizations in accordance with applicable laws.
